Privacy Policy

Pilgrims Camino Companion

Effective Date: February 20, 2026

Pilgrims Camino Companion does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Overview

Pilgrims Camino Companion is a mobile application designed to guide pilgrims along the Camino de Santiago. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the app works.

Data Collection

This app does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.
  • We do not track your location or collect GPS data.
  • We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking technologies.
  • We do not collect usage data or device information.

Data Storage

All app content — including route information, stage details, reflections, accommodation listings, and restaurant data — is bundled directly within the app and stored locally on your device.

Any personal data you enter into the app, such as journal entries and stage completion progress, is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data is never transmitted to any server or third party.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app uses Apple's MapKit framework to display maps, which is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Internet Connectivity

The app is designed to work offline. An internet connection is only used to load map tiles through Apple Maps. No personal data is sent over the network by this app.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
