Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work without an internet connection?

Yes. All route content, stage reflections, historical information, accommodation listings, and restaurant details are stored directly on your device. The only feature that benefits from a connection is the interactive map, which loads map tiles from Apple Maps. Once you've viewed an area, those tiles are cached and may remain available offline.

Which Camino routes are included?

The app includes seven routes: the Camino Francés, Camino Portugués, Camino del Norte, Camino Primitivo, Vía de la Plata, Camino Inglés, and Camino Finisterre — with a total of 114 stages.

Where is my journal data stored?

Journal entries and stage completion progress are saved locally on your device only. They are not uploaded to any server or cloud service. If you delete the app, this data will be removed from your device.

Can I listen to the reflections instead of reading them?

Yes. Each stage reflection and content section includes an audio button that reads the text aloud using your device's built-in text-to-speech. You can play, pause, and stop at any time.

How do I track my progress?

On each stage detail screen, you'll find a "Mark Complete" button. Tap it to record that stage as finished. Your progress is shown as a visual bar on the route overview screen.

The app isn't working as expected. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store. If you're still experiencing problems, please email us with a description of the issue and your device model, and we'll help resolve it.

About the App

Pilgrims Camino Companion is a daily guide for pilgrims walking the Way of St. James. It provides stage-by-stage reflections, historical context, sacred site information, practical pilgrim details, and an interactive map — all designed to enrich your journey.
